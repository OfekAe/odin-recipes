The broth of fruit bat soup is made by washing a bat and throwing it in boiling water, fur included! Boil the bat stock with lemon grass, galangal, kaffir leaves, and vegetables until it simmers. Diced the bat meat without the bones and put it in until it cooks. Season with salt, sugar, and white pepper. And squash lime on top of it to brighten its flavor, yum yum! Serve it hot with rice.
